About Us

BANIMATION is a small animation studio that specializes in 2D animation. We are a team of 3 artists who are passionate about creating animations that bring stories to life. Our goal is to create animations that are not only visually appealing but also emotionally engaging. We believe that animation is a powerful medium that can be used to tell stories in a unique and creative way. We are dedicated to creating high-quality animations that captivate and inspire our audience.

Founder

BANIMATION was founded by Akein Bantin, an artist and animator with a passion for storytelling. Akein has been drawing and animating since he was a child and has always been fascinated by the world of animation. He started BANIMATION with the goal of creating animations that inspire and entertain people. Akein is dedicated to creating animations that push the boundaries of creativity and storytelling.
